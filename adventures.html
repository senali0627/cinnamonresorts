Cinnamon Peeling & Toddy Tapping experience

Cinnamon is endemic to Sri Lanka and it was discovered in our island by the Portuguese. Over time, cinnamon cultivation and export became one of our prime assets as Sri Lanka produces 90% of the world's supply of true cinnamon. This spice is used in savoury foods, has a fragrant aroma, is incredibly beneficial for health, and frequently used in traditional Ayurveda remedies. Cinnamon cultivation is a unique and scarcely known process. It is truly a one-of-a-kind experience that is both interesting and authentic. While you are visiting the cinnamon factory, you will see the cinnamon peelers at work. Sometimes the entire family gets involved! You will learn about the uses and healing properties of cinnamon and its grading process. As a special treat, a cinnamon tea tasting session can be arranged upon request.

  • Duration of excursion : Minimum 2-3 hours
  • Location : Uragaha
  • Distance from hotel : 50 km (approx.)
  • Time of departure : 9.00 am to 3.00 pm
  • Number of persons : 6 pax

Pottery

Indulge in the art of pottery and unleash your creative spirit with our immersive pottery experience at cinnamon hotels. Designed for both beginners and seasoned artists, our pottery workshop offers a unique opportunity to engage in a hands-on artistic journey during your stay. Under the guidance of our skilled pottery instructors, you'll step into a world of clay, where imagination takes shape. Immerse yourself in the therapeutic process of molding clay into beautiful pottery pieces, while discovering the joy of creating something with your own hands.

Our well-equipped pottery studio provides a serene and inspiring ambiance, complete with pottery wheels, sculpting tools, and a variety of glazes and paints to add color and texture to your masterpieces.

The pottery experience begins with an introduction to the art of pottery, where you'll learn the techniques, tools, and materials involved. Our instructors will guide you through the process, providing demonstrations and offering personalized tips to help you bring your artistic vision to life

Whether you're seeking a unique experience to unwind and tap into your artistic abilities or looking to bond with friends and family over a shared creative endeavor, our pottery experience at Cinnamon hotels promises to be a memorable and enriching activity during your stay. Immerse yourself in the world of pottery and leave with not only beautiful creations but also a renewed sense of inspiration and accomplishment

Puppetry

Puppetry is a folk art that is still popular in our villages. We invite the makers of these puppets to pass on their skills to you. Are you making a face right now? The happy face, we mean. Join in then! All materials will be provided by us. All you need to bring along is enthusiasm

Our expert instructors will guide you through the art of puppet-making, teaching you various techniques and sharing insider tips to help you create a unique character. Learn the art of shaping and attaching limbs, sewing intricate costumes, and adding expressive features that will bring your puppet to life.
